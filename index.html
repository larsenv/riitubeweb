RiiTube: Watch YouTube on your Wii

Welcome

RiiTube allows you to watch YouTube videos on your Wii using WiiMC, a homebrew app that functions as a media player.

How to Install

  1. Make sure your console is modded and has the Homebrew Channel. Visit wii.hacks.guide for tutorials.
  2. Download WiiMC-SS, our highly recommended version of WiiMC.
  3. Launch WiiMC-SS from the Homebrew Channel.
  4. Select the Network icon (to the right of the disc icon) at the top to start streaming.

Features

  • Supports YouTube, Dailymotion, and Vimeo
  • Video thumbnails for easy browsing
  • Works on any homebrewed Wii
  • Completely advertisement-free
  • Simple plug-and-play experience

FAQ

RiiTube does not work, I am getting an error on WiiMC

It's possible there's a problem with the specific video. Try another one. If issues persist, the server might be undergoing maintenance. Join our Discord for real-time status updates.

How does RiiTube work?

We utilize yt-dlp and serve videos through specialized proxies to ensure compatibility with legacy hardware like the Wii.

Does it work with the official YouTube Channel or the Internet Channel?

No, RiiTube is specifically designed to work within WiiMC.

Why can't I fast forward or rewind?

Seeking is currently unsupported due to the way videos are streamed to the media player.
